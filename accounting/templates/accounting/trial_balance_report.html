The Trial Balance lists the ending balance of every account in your Chart of Accounts. It is the primary tool for detecting errors: Total Debits must always equal Total Credits.

What to look for:

Asset and Expense accounts usually have Debit balances.
Liability, Equity, and Revenue accounts usually have Credit balances.
