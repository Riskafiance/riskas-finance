Journal Vouchers

General Ledger Adjustments

Journal Vouchers (JVs) are the raw entries into your accounting books. While Invoices and Bills create entries automatically, you use Manual JVs for:

  • Corrections: Fixing errors in previous transactions.
  • Non-Cash Items: Recording Depreciation or Amortization.
  • Adjustments: End-of-month accruals or deferrals.
  • Opening Balances: Setting up initial account values.

The Golden Rule:

Total Debits must always equal Total Credits. The system will not let you post an unbalanced entry.
