**Inventory** represents the finished goods you currently hold in stock, waiting to be sold. Unlike Expenses (money gone), Inventory is an Asset because it still has value.

Financial Impact: The "Total Value" shown below is added to your Total Assets on the Balance Sheet. It only becomes an Expense (Cost of Goods Sold) when you actually sell it.
