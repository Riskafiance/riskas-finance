Expenses

Cash Outflow

This module tracks direct payments made from your bank accounts or credit cards. Unlike Purchase Orders (which are promises to pay), Expenses represent money that has already left your business.

Automation: Saving an expense automatically creates a Journal Entry that credits your Bank/Cash account and debits the selected Expense account.
